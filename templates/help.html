How Donations Work

Meal-Link connects restaurants, NGOs, and volunteers to reduce food waste and feed those in need. The process is simple:

  • Step 1: Restaurants post surplus food using the donation form.
  • Step 2: NGOs or volunteers browse available meals and claim them.
  • Step 3: Pickups are coordinated, ensuring safe and timely delivery.

All donations are time-bound to ensure freshness and food safety.

Safety & Hygiene Guidelines

  • Ensure food is stored at safe temperatures until pickup.
  • Use clean, sealed containers for packaging meals.
  • Avoid donating perishable food older than 4 hours (without refrigeration).
  • Clearly label vegetarian/non-vegetarian items.
